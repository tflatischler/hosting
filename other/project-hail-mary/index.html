The Mission

The sun is fading. Earth's only hope is one ordinary man.

Dr Ryland Grace wakes up aboard the spaceship Hail Mary with no idea who he is or how he got there. He is completely alone — and nearly twelve light-years from home.

As his memories slowly return, he begins to understand the terrible truth: a mysterious organism called the Astrophage is draining the sun's energy, and the Earth is running out of time. Ryland has been sent on a desperate mission to find a solution before it is too late.

What he discovers along the way — and who he meets — will change everything he thought he knew about science, courage, and what it truly means to be human.
